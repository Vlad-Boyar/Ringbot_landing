FAQ

How much does it cost?
First week is free — no risk trial.

After that you pick a plan:
• Basic — £69 / month (200 minutes, then £0.30/min)
• Pro — £149 / month (500 minutes, then £0.25/min)
• VIP — £399 / month (1500 minutes, then £0.20/min)

You can upgrade or downgrade at any time.
Can you use my existing phone number?
Yes. We give you the assistant’s number. You enable call forwarding from your number to that one. Switch it on/off any time — at night, on weekends, or when you’re busy. Or just leave it on and enjoy hands-free call handling.
How many calls can it handle?
The assistant can answer multiple calls at once. Limits depend on your plan:
• Basic — up to 5 concurrent calls
• Pro — up to 10 concurrent calls
• VIP — unlimited concurrent calls
What integrations are available?
Out of the box: Telegram notifications, Google Sheets logging, and custom webhooks. We can also connect to most CRMs with an API.
Voices & accents
Plenty of high-quality voices with different accents. We can even clone your voice — or your receptionist’s — if you want it to sound exactly like your brand.
Notifications
Right after each call we send you a Telegram message with the most important details. No need to waste time on long calls — you get the key summary instantly.
How do we get started?
To start, fill out the request form — we’ll get in touch shortly to confirm the details. Or call our RingBot and leave a request through it. Same-day setup for simple flows.
Can I pause or cancel any time?
Yes — month-to-month. Pause or cancel whenever you like; your data and settings remain intact for when you return.
